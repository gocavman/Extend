## Size Comparison

Most emojis render at similar sizes, but:
- Double emojis (⬆️⬇️) take up more space - about 1.5x
- Single emojis (⬆️) render at same size as current
- Symbols (⚡, 💨) render at same size as current
- Complex emojis (🎯, 🔫) render at same size as current

---

## Readability at Small Sizes

**Clear at Small Grid Sizes:**
- ↕️ ✅ (current)
- ⬆️⬇️ ✅ (chevrons)
- ⚡ ✅ (lightning)
- 🎯 ✅ (target)

**Potentially Unclear at Small Sizes:**
- ➰ ⚠️ (curved arrow)
- 🟵⬆️ ⚠️ (combined symbols - might overlap)

## Color Contrast

**Against Common Background Colors:**
- ↕️ ✅ Good visibility
- ⬆️⬇️ ✅ Good visibility
- ⚡ ✅ Good visibility
- 🎯 ✅ Good visibility
- 💨 ✅ Good visibility

### If You Want: Clarity & Professionalism
